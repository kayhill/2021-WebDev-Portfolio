About

Hello! My name is Kayliegh.

I specialize in creating websites for small businesses seeking greater visibility on the web. From design to launch, I love to work with owners to identify goals and bring them to life through code.

Depending on the project and needs of the client, I work with Content Management Systems and/or DIY code to build clean, responsive websites with a focus on SEO and UX. I configure the back-end structure of sites and manage their servers and databases.

In between clients, I enjoy building web applications using JavaScript and Python. Lately I've been diving into cloud-based architecture and blockchain technology.

Technical Toolkit

Backend Python, Django, PostgreSQL, Cloud Servers (AWS EC2, Lightsail)
Frontend JavaScript, CSS, HTML, WordPress
Other GitHub, Linux command-line, Heroku
